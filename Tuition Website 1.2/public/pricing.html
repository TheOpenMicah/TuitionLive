Pricing

We are committed to making high-quality tuition accessible to everyone. Our base rate is £30 per hour.

Discounted rates are available for families of children with EAL (English as an Additional Language) or SEND (Special Educational Needs and Disabilities), and for those in lower income brackets. If eligible, the rate is £20 per hour.

Group and sibling bookings are also available at reduced rates. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs and eligibility for discounts.
