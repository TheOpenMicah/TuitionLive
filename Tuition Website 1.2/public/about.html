About Eleanor Private Tuition

With years of experience and a genuine passion for literature, Eleanor offers a nurturing and effective learning environment. Her teaching philosophy is centered around understanding each student's unique interests and learning style to create tailored English lessons that inspire and motivate.

Her approach is perfect for home-educating families seeking to foster a deep and lasting love for language and storytelling, moving beyond the curriculum to explore the creative world of words.

Whether your child is looking to spark their creativity, build reading confidence, or develop core literacy skills, Eleanor's bespoke tuition is designed to support and inspire every learner.
